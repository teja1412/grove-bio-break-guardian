# 🌳 Grove — Bio-Break Guardian

Grove is a wellness-focused productivity companion that helps users build healthier work habits.

Instead of showing generic reminders, Grove visualizes your wellbeing through a living tree that responds to your behavior. As you work, your tree grows and flourishes. Ignore breaks for too long, and the grove begins to wilt.

## ✨ Why Grove?

Many professionals spend hours in front of screens without taking proper breaks. Existing productivity tools focus on maximizing work output, but very few encourage sustainable work habits.

Grove was designed around a simple idea:

> Your digital environment should care about your wellbeing as much as your productivity.

## 🌱 Features

### Focus & Break Cycles

* 30-minute focus sessions
* 10-minute recovery breaks
* Automatic cycle management

### Living Tree Visualization

* Tree health reflects user behavior
* Healthy habits keep the grove thriving
* Ignored breaks visibly impact the tree
* Falling leaves and visual decay signal fatigue

### Gamification

* XP system
* Tree levels
* Achievements
* Daily streak tracking
* Progressive tree evolution

### Intelligent Presence Detection

* Automatically pauses focus sessions when the user steps away
* Prevents inaccurate productivity tracking
* Resumes when activity returns

### Accessibility

* Voice-guided reminders
* Screen-reader support
* Visual and non-visual health indicators
* Desktop notifications

### Immersive Experience

* Animated fireflies
* Dynamic tree growth
* Seasonal visual progression
* Level-up celebrations
